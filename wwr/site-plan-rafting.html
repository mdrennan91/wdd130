Overview

Purpose

Our website will build brand awareness by showcasing the unique experiences we offer. It will serve as the platform for introducing our brand to new audiences, and positioning ourselves as a leader in the white water rafting industry. In order to drive revenue, our website will be designed to encourage bookings and reservations for our white water rafting trips. It will be easy to use and informative while also providing potential customers with all the information they need to make an informed decision. Our website will provide useful resources for people who are interested in white water rafting such as pictures, videos, frequently asked questions, and customer testimonials. Overall, the purpose of our website is to create a positive and memorable experience for anyone who visits, whether they book a trip or simply want to learn more about white water rafting. By acheiving these goals, we hope to establish ourselves as the go-to destination for anyone seeking adventure in the great outdoors.

Audience

Our target audience will be adults between the ages of 25 and 45 who are physically fit and seeking a challenge. However, we also welcome families and individuals who are not within our target range of ages. Our audience is interested in outdoor adventure and travel. They will most likely enjoy activities including hiking, camping, swimming, rock climbing, and fishing. They may also be interested in environmental consevation and sustainability. Our target audience comes from a diverse range of financial situations. We welcome anyone who is passionate about adventure and outdoor experiences. While some customers may have a middle to high income level and are willing to invest in unique experiences, we also offer affordable options for those who are on a tighter budget. They want to experience the thrill of white water rafting while surrounded by the natural beauty of mountains and rivers. They want to create memories with their loved ones while challenging themselves physically and mentally. Our audience also wants to feel safe during their adventure with our experienced guides and instructors. They will primarily be accessing our website through mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets, as well as through desktop and laptop computers. We need to ensure that our website is optimized for all devices with easy to use navigation.

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to serve as a platform where I can showcase my photography portfolio and attract potential clients seeking high quality photos of their cars. I'm here to help celebrate the artisty behind every vehicle, delivering images that inspire others to find their automotive passion.

Audience

The intended audience will be car enthusiasts who are passionate about the artisty and beauty of their cars, and want to share their vision with others on social media. I also offer my services to anyone who is interested in selling their cars, such as private sellers, collectors, dealerships, or auction houses.

About Me

About Me
My name is Mitchel Drennan and I am located in the state of Washinton. I'm an avid photographer with a relentless passion for capturing the essence of vehicles. Over the past seven years, I have immersed myself in the art of photography, constantly refining my skills and exploring various genres. However, it was automotive photography that truly ignited my creative spark and allowed me to fully express my vision. With an unwavering love for anything on wheels, I am dedicated to bringing these magnificent machines to life through my lens. From the sleek lines of exotics to the raw power of muscle cars, I thrive on capturing their unique personalities and showcasing them in all their glory. It's a fulfilling journey that never ceases to amaze me. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to explore different branches of photography, including portraits and nature. These experiences have contributed to my growth as an artist, providing me with a diverse skill set that complements my automotive work. Drawing inspiration from the natural world and the human spirit, I bring a fresh perspective to every shoot. I have collaborated with prestigious car clubs, sharing my passion with fellow enthusiasts. Whether it's an exhilarating nighttime shoot, capturing the motion of a moving vehicle, or curating a themed photoshoot to showcase its personality, I'm committed to delivering captivating images that exceed expectations.

While I am based in the state of Washington, I am thrilled to offer my services beyond this region. I love exploring new locations, discovering breathtaking scenery, and capturing stunning automotive moments in different settings. Whether you have a scenic spot in mind or a particular event you'd like me to cover, I am available to travel for photoshoots. From coastal drives to scenic mountain roads, I'm willing to go the extra mile to ensure your vision is brought to life. Please keep in mind that travel expenses, including transportation and accommodation, may apply for shoots located outside the eastern Washington area. However, rest assured that I will work closely with you to make the process as seamless as possible, ensuring that every detail is taken care of and your experience remains exceptional. Don't let distance limit your imagination. Let's collaborate and create stunning automotive imagery, no matter where your inspiration takes us. Contact me to discuss your ideas and let's embark on an exciting photographic adventure together.

Equipment
When it comes to equipment, I rely on the exceptional capabilities of my trusted Nikon D7200. Alongside a collection of carefully selected lenses, I ensure that every detail is impeccably captured. To further enhance the quality and bring out the full potential of each photograph, I professionally edit all my images using Adobe Lightroom, adding that extra touch of magic.
